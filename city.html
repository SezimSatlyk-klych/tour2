Modern Astana (now Nur-Sultan) is the capital of Kazakhstan, known for its stunning futuristic architecture. Built from scratch in the 1990s, it quickly transformed from a small town into a bustling metropolis. The city showcases Kazakhstan's economic growth with iconic landmarks like the Baiterek Tower and Khan Shatyr, a giant tent-shaped shopping complex. Astana is also a cultural hub, home to the National Museum and the Palace of Peace, designed by Norman Foster. Despite its extreme weather, with cold winters and hot summers, the city remains a thriving center of politics, culture, and modern living.

Recommendations

Baiterek Tower: A modern symbol of the city offering panoramic views of Astana.

Khan Shatyr: An iconic entertainment center with shops, restaurants, and even an indoor beach.

National Museum of the Republic of Kazakhstan: Discover Kazakhstan’s rich history and cultural artifacts.
